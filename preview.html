FAQ

Things you might be wondering.

Will Palate kill my battery?

No. We use the iPhone's lowest-power location signals (significant location changes), not continuous GPS. Most users see no measurable battery impact.

How accurate is the detection?

Very good for sit-down spots; pretty good for fast casual; sometimes wrong for big food courts or dense streets. That's why we always ask before saving — and let you pick the right place.

Is it free?

Free during beta. We'll share details on long-term plans before anything changes — there'll always be a free tier.

What about Android?

Coming after iOS. We want one platform polished before splitting focus.

Why does Palate ask before saving?

Because walking past a restaurant isn't the same as eating there. One tap from you is the only way to know for sure — and it keeps the data honest.

What does Palate do with my location?

We use it only to find what's near you when you're considering a meal. We never share location with restaurants or sell it. You can pause or wipe it anytime. Read the full policy.
